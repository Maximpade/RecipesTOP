The Cheesiest Beef Quesadillas

A crispy, golden tortilla filled with seasoned ground beef and melted cheese, perfect for a quick and delicious meal. Serve with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp taco seasoning
  • 4 large tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef and mix with taco seasoning.
  2. Place beef and cheese on half of each tortilla.
  3. Fold tortillas and cook on a pan until golden brown.
  4. Slice and serve with salsa. Enjoy!
