Bacon Burger

The best bacon burger (according to my cat)

This bacon burger is easy to make and packed with flavor. Crispy bacon, a juicy seasoned beef patty, and melted cheddar come together on a buttery toasted bun. Add your favorite toppings and condiments, then enjoy a simple, delicious homemade burger!

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef (80/20)
  • 4 slices bacon
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp butter (for toasting buns)
  • Lettuce, tomato, pickles (optional)
  • Mayo, ketchup, mustard (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook bacon until crispy, then set aside.
  2. Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. form patties and cook on medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes per side.
  4. Melt cheese on patties in the last minute of cooking.
  5. Toast buns with butter until golden.
  6. Assemble burgers with bacon, patties, toppings, and condiments.
  7. Plate, serve and enjoy!
